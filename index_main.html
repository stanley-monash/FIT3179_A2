This bubble chart shows the relationship between GFP per capita and the happiness score, as well as total population for each country and its continent. On top of that some text annotations are made to distinguish well known countries. Observing the bubble chart, we can identify that generally European countries have higher GDP per capita and have higher happiness scores. Whereas Asian countries have generally lower GDP per capita (with a few exceptions), tend to have relatively lower happiness score.
